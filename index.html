Who am I?

I'm a recent college grad that began his first development position nearly 6 months ago. As a computer scientist, I’d argue that there’s nothing quite like the peaks and troughs that come with programming. The great crushing despair when I can’t solve a problem paired with the Everest-sized highs when I finally get it right is what I enjoy more than just about anything else. I’ve taken a lot of computer science courses, finished a couple personal coding projects, and am currently working on a real world software system. No matter the type of application, I can’t wait to meet the challenge.
